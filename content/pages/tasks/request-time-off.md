# Request Time Off

Use this guide to submit a request for vacation, sick leave, or personal time off.

## Overview

All time off requests must be submitted through the HR portal and approved by your direct manager. Requests should be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance when possible.

## Types of Leave

- **Vacation**: Planned time off for personal activities
- **Sick Leave**: For illness or medical appointments
- **Personal Time**: For personal matters that cannot be scheduled during non-work hours

## Important Notes

- Manager approval is required for all time off requests
- Certain dates may be restricted due to business needs
- Check with your manager before requesting time off during peak periods
