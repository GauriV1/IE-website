# Time Off Policy

This policy outlines the procedures and guidelines for requesting and using time off.

## Accrual Rates

Full-time employees accrue 1.25 days per month. Part-time employees accrue PTO on a pro-rated basis.

## Request Process

All time off requests must be submitted through the HR portal and approved by your direct manager.

## Blackout Dates

Certain dates may be restricted due to business needs. Check with your manager before requesting.

## Carryover Policy

Unused PTO can be carried over up to 5 days to the following year. Any PTO in excess of 5 days will be forfeited.
